Nested Lists


Several tasks can be separated based on their categories. Let's see how we can make use of nested Lists to do it.

  • Work
    1. Attend a meeting
    2. Create documentation

  • Chores
    1. Laundry
    2. Prepare meal

  • Self-improvement/Health
    1. Finish a book
    2. Go on a walk

  • Leisure
    1. Which movie
    2. Boking
